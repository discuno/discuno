Starting at the University of Michigan is exciting, intimidating, and full of decisions you don’t want to make alone. Discuno connects you with mentors who have already navigated Ann Arbor, from freshman orientation to graduate school applications. Use this guide to ask smart questions before move-in day so you can hit the ground running.

## Essential Topics to Cover

### Academics

- Which introductory courses fill up fastest?
- What do professors actually expect in first-year seminars?
- How do you balance lectures, labs, and discussion sections without burning out?

### Campus Life

- Where should first-years live if they want a quieter dorm?
- Which student orgs help you meet friends quickly?
- What are the underrated traditions every Wolverine should know?

### Career Prep

- How early should you visit the career center or attend companies’ info sessions?
- What does a successful freshman-year resume look like?
- Which local internships or research labs are open to first-year students?

### Financial Wellness

- How do meal plans compare to cooking in off-campus apartments?
- What are the go-to resources for scholarships, emergency funds, and part-time jobs?
- Are there hidden costs (lab fees, course packets, transportation) to budget for?
