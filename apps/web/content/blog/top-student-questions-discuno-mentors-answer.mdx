Students turn to Discuno when they need practical answers that go beyond search results. Our mentors include current students, alumni, and industry professionals who love sharing the playbooks they wish they had sooner. If you’re wondering when Discuno can help, start with these questions—asked every semester by students just like you.

## College Admissions & Decisions

- _“How do I compare financial aid packages from multiple schools?”_  
  Mentors walk through award letters line by line and surface hidden costs like lab fees or travel expenses.

- _“What should I know before committing to a specific campus?”_  
  Chat with mentors from schools like Michigan, UCLA, or Georgia Tech to understand housing, academic rigor, and social life before you say yes.

## First-Year Survival

- _“Which classes should I take in my first semester?”_  
  Mentors explain how to balance gen-ed requirements, major prerequisites, and professor reputations.

- _“How do I make friends or join the right clubs?”_  
  Get advice on welcome week strategies, student org fairs, and community-building without burning out.

## Academic Growth

- _“I’m thinking about switching majors—what’s the process?”_  
  Mentors detail timeline checkpoints, advisor conversations, and credit transfer pitfalls.

- _“How do I stay on track in intense courses?”_  
  Learn study hacks, office hour etiquette, and note-taking systems tailored to STEM, humanities, or creative programs.

## Career & Internship Prep

- _“When should I start applying for internships?”_  
  Mentors share recruiting calendars for tech, finance, healthcare, and media so you beat the rush.

- _“How do I stand out in interviews?”_  
  Practice behavioral questions, whiteboard challenges, or portfolio walkthroughs with mentors who hire in your field.

## Life Logistics

- _“Can I manage part-time work alongside classes?”_  
  Mentors discuss time management, on-campus job options, and budgeting tools.

- _“What’s the best way to plan for off-campus housing?”_  
  Get insider tips on neighborhoods, lease negotiation, and roommate matching.

## Graduation & Beyond

- _“How do I negotiate my first job offer?”_  
  Mentors role-play conversations, compare benefits packages, and help you make confident decisions.

- _“Should I go straight to grad school?”_  
  Hear from alumni who weighed gap years, fellowships, or immediate employment.
