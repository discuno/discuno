## Why Peer Mentorship Matters

At Discuno, we believe that some of the best advice comes from those who have recently walked in your shoes. Our verified student mentors understand the challenges you're facing because they've overcome them themselves.

Research shows that peer mentorship can:

- Improve academic performance
- Increase retention rates
- Build confidence and self-efficacy
- Expand professional networks
- Provide relatable role models
